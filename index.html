About Me

I'm an interaction designer with a technical background: one of those “designers who code.” I like to make things (mainly software things), and I aspire to make things that solve real societal problems. I'm also finishing up studying human-computer interaction, computer science, and communication design at Carnegie Mellon University.

I'm currently a interning with the Mozilla Firefox UX Design team. Previously, I worked at Stik as a front-end developer/designer and LearnBoost as a generalist developer.
